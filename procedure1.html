Procedure 1: Technical Support Procedures

This document provides a step-by-step guide on handling technical support requests.

  1. Step 1: Assess the nature of the technical issue and categorize it (e.g., software, hardware, network).
  2. Step 2: Gather relevant information from the user, including screenshots, error messages, and device details.
  3. Step 3: Provide initial troubleshooting steps (e.g., restarting the device, checking connections).
  4. Step 4: Escalate to a higher support tier if the issue remains unresolved after initial troubleshooting.
  5. Step 5: Document the issue and resolution steps in the support log for future reference.
