Sales Order Processing Guide

This guide provides instructions for processing sales orders, ensuring they are handled accurately and efficiently.

Step-by-Step Guide:

  1. Order Receipt: Receive the order details from the sales team or directly from the client.
  2. Verification: Confirm the order details, including quantities, prices, and any applicable discounts.
  3. Order Entry: Enter the order into the system, ensuring all data is correct and up-to-date.
  4. Confirmation and Payment: Confirm the order with the client and arrange for payment processing.
  5. Shipping and Fulfillment: Coordinate with the fulfillment team to package and ship the order.
  6. Completion and Follow-Up: Update the order status to complete and follow up with the client to ensure satisfaction.
