About TechEon

TechEon was founded with the vision of empowering businesses and individuals through innovative technology solutions. Our focus is on streamlining operations and providing cutting-edge support to our clients.

This website was created as a hub for Techsupport agents to access critical information while assisting Global Sales Consultancy (GSC) in managing their technical needs. Here, you can find procedural documents, support forms, and helpful links that aid in daily tasks.

We aim to deliver reliable, efficient, and effective service support to ensure that GSC’s operations run smoothly.

How to Use This Website

  1. Navigate through the menu to find the documents and forms you need.
  2. Click on the “Support Documents” section to access procedural documents that guide daily technical operations.
  3. Visit the “Request Form” page to submit service requests. Simply fill in the required fields and hit submit.
  4. For any help or inquiries, click the email hyperlink below to contact the TechEon Helpdesk.

If you encounter any issues or need further assistance, feel free to contact us at ithelp@techsupport.com.
