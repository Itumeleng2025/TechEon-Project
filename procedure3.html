Marketing Request Handling Guide

This guide covers the procedures for handling marketing requests, ensuring they align with the company’s branding and marketing goals.

Step-by-Step Guide:

  1. Request Submission: Receive the marketing request, ensuring it includes necessary details such as campaign objectives, target audience, and deadline.
  2. Initial Assessment: Review the request to determine feasibility and any initial resources required.
  3. Approval Process: Submit the request for approval by the marketing manager and obtain necessary sign-offs.
  4. Execution: Implement the marketing request, coordinating with relevant teams (design, content, etc.) to produce and deploy materials.
  5. Monitoring and Reporting: Track the request’s progress and provide updates to the requester, ensuring alignment with the original goals.
  6. Completion and Review: Once completed, review the results and gather feedback for continuous improvement.
