Support FAQ

To escalate a technical issue, ensure you have completed all standard troubleshooting steps. Document your findings, include relevant error logs, and submit a report through the escalation form on our support platform. If the issue remains unresolved, contact your direct supervisor for further assistance.

Begin by cross-referencing the order details with the customer’s purchase history. Verify the product codes, quantities, and billing information. Finally, ensure the payment method is valid and authorized before processing the order in the system.

If a request is outside the scope, politely inform the customer and provide alternative resources if possible. Document the request and refer it to the relevant department for additional guidance if necessary.
